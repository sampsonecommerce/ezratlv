The experience

An exceptional hosting experience.

The entire space is yours. We host only one event per evening, to guarantee complete privacy for you and your guests.

Our attention goes to the small details, from the design of the space, through the lighting, to the perfect soundtrack. We created an environment built for elegant hosting, in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

We offer a complete hosting package with a curated chef's menu, an open cocktail bar and uncompromising service. A seamless experience that lets you simply show up and enjoy.

Open seven days a week, for daytime or evening events.
